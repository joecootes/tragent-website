Trust

Connecting your inbox
is a big ask. Here are the rules.

Tragent only touches customer enquiries. Everything else in your Google account is off limits, and everything it sends is visible in the app.

Tragent can

  • Read new customer enquiries as they arrive
  • Send replies on your behalf
  • Track customer conversations as jobs
  • Schedule and send follow-ups

Tragent cannot

  • Access Google Drive, Calendar or Photos
  • Read emails from before you connected
  • Send quotes, prices or technical advice
  • Email anyone who is not a customer enquiry
